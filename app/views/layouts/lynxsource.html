our mission

Lynx is a movement to help you build deeply meaningful professional relationships.

SEEKERS

You have an amazing idea for a project. You're looking for an exciting new role in a stellar company. You're looking for a rockstar candidate to fill a role. Or perhaps you'd like to finally meet the right mentor. We can help you connect with the right people and the right opportunities.

CONNECTORS

You love to connect people who should know each other. You build the community and are an integral part of curating the interconnectedness of your network. We empower connectors to make more informed introductions that lead to better results.

ORGANIZERS

You bring together the community. You work hard to to make your community tight-knit and help it to thrive by producing quality events. We give event organizers tools to engage their communities in real-time at events and gather valuable feedback from members.

Learn something new

Each month we invite a distinguished speaker from the local community to present their work on exciting new advancements. Our speakers are passionate about their topics and passing on their knowledge to the community. If you have a speaker that you'd really like to hear from, we'd be happy to reach out to them for a future Lynx event.

Our goal is to make everyone feel welcome

Lynx is about building community during every one of our events. We encourage people to go beyond small talk to really get to know the other aspiring professionals in the room. The best connections are formed through genuine conversation, and you'd be surprised at how much you can learn just by spending the time to find out what each person has to say about their passions, their work, and their career aspirations.

Become a Lynx volunteer

Part of being a Lynx member is the ownership of the event and the community. We encourage you to help us create experiences around the topics that you're interested in exploring. Your time will be very valuably spent in fostering the Lynx community as well as putting yourself in a great position to meet and work with many quality individuals.
